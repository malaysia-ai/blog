What is Face Verification

You’re sitting at home. You want to apply for a visa for an upcoming vacation. You open your laptop, or pick up your mobile phone, and log on to the government visa service. You use the device camera to scan your driver’s license or passport to prove your identity. You then scan your face. Facial verification technology confirms that your physical face matches the one in the ID document, and that you are real and completing this application right now. -- iproov

Based on above statement we need two image, one from your driver's licence/passport another one is your selfie image so that the system can compare.

Conclusion

Human do face recognition by comparing feature of a person that is stored in their memory and compare with the person in front of them. That's why we did not do similar technique being done like Image classification for Imagenet dataset for example. With comparing we don't need to have fixed number of class

Glossary

  1. Embedding = Vector consist of the image information that is learned by the model(similar image will be near to each other). You can think of embedding like array of one dimension [1,2] and [1,3]. If you plot them they will be near to each other compare to [10,200].Usually embedding got lot more column like 256 and above not two column(x and y) like above which make it more expressive.
  2. Distance = measure of how much alike two data objects are https://dataaspirant.com/five-most-popular-similarity-measures-implementation-in-python/
